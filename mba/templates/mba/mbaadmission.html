Admissions – MBA Programme 2020-2022

KVM Institute of Management Studies invites applications for admission to its two years Full Time MBA Program – Batch 2020-22

Total intake: 60 seats

Date of issue of application forms: 01/01/2020

Eligibility

A pass in degree examination with minimum 50% marks in aggregate for Arts/Commerce /Science/ Engineering. Final year students are also eligible to apply
Good score in any Management Aptitude Test- C-MAT / CAT/KMAT

Selection

The selection of candidates is purely based on merit. Admission to the MBA programme is determined by the scores obtained by the candidate in the admission test, group discussion,personal interview and a consistent academic record .

How to Apply

Application form and prospectus can be obtained directly either at the college office or at the KVM Trust office by paying Rs 500/-. Application form and prospectus can be downloaded from our website, www.kvmce.in and the filled application form can be forwarded to the college office along with a demand draft of Rs. 500/- in favour of KVM College of Engg & IT, payable at Cherthala
