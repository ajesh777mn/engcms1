Approval & Recognition

KVM College of Engineering & Information Technology started in 2001-2002 academic year. It is approved by the All India Council for Technical Education, affiliated to APJ Abdul Kalam Technological University and is recognized by Cochin University of Science & Technology. Currently the college offers B.Tech - CSE, MCA and MBA (affiliated to Kerala Technological University) and MSc Biotechnology programs recognized by Cochin University of Science & Technology.
