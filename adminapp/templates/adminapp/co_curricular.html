Co-Curricular

The students play a major role in running the college through their association. The Students association acts as a focal point for student's life in the campus and organizes social functions and sports activities. This is the voice of the students at the institution inside the campus.

The college has fairly good facilities for outdoor games and indoor games. A wide range of activities are organized by the students through the advice and support of the faculty. Cultural programmes and Hostel / College Day programmes make life in the campus a memorable experience.
