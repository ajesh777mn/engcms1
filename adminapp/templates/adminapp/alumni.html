Alumni

The Inauguration of Alumni Association of the department of Computer Application was held on 30th April 2016. The ceremony was inaugurated by Dr. S V Muruga Prasad, Principal, KVM CE & IT by lighting the lamp. Mr.Babu. B, General Manager did the felicitation. The formal part of the programme concluded with the vote of thanks by Dr Nageshwara Rao , HOD, Department of Computer Applications. He thanked all the invited guests and salient supporters for gracing the occasion. The programme continued with the entertainment by the current students and ex-students.

It was time again for nostalgia, as around 55 ex-students from different batches of the department gathered to relive old memories. A few of the alumni shared their experience as well as interesting moments of campus life to the audience.

In this function the department announced the best student award in 2013-2016 batch. Ms. Anumol Mathew was selected as the best student and she was awarded for the same by the principal.
